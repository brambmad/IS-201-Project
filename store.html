Key To A Long-Lasting Tan Before and After Tan Prep

Before Tan

1. Exfoliate and Moisturize: 24 hours before

2. Wax/Shave: 48 hours before

3. Don't Apply Moisturiser, Perfume, Deodorant, or Makeup: Day Of Appointment

After Tan

1. Wear Dark Loose Clothing: 24 hours after

2. Shower: without soap 6-12 hours after

3. Moisturize Every Day and Stay Hydrated

4. Avoid Excessive Swimming, Exfoliation, & Sun Exposure
